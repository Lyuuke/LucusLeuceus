The texture set coded "zhongqiu" contains seasonable textures for the
Sinospheric festival Zhongqiu (Mid-Autumn), namely:

* Osmanthus flowers - fragrant blossoms around the season.

* Mooncakes.

* Crab, more specifically Eriocheir sinensis.

This texture set was created for practice.
